While we have blocks at two hotels, we highly recommend the Hampton Inn. While the Hilton is only an extra block or two away, it seems that the Hampton Inn is the better deal. Registry

We realize just how incredibly fortunate we are: We are happy and healthy; we are able to live as we like and buy what we want. While we’ve registered at Amazon and Bed, Bath & Beyond for anyone who would like to buy us a gift, we would like to ask you to consider donating to either Leukemia & Lymphoma Society or the Tabitha Fund.

The Leukemia & Lymphoma Society (LLS) is focused on finding cures and better treatments for blood cancer patients. The research they fund can help patients live longer, better lives. Personally speaking, there will be holes in our wedding because of cancer, people who should’ve been there to share it with us who were taken way too soon. While no amount of research can bring them back, it can maybe stop others from suffering the same.

The Tabitha Fund is for a dog surrendered to the vet that we use, Griffin Animal Hospital. In addition to being great vets and really caring people, GAH will take in animals in great need. In February 2015, they took in a dog named Tabitha that had been abused by her owner and needed a number of surgeries. While they can cover most of the cost of the items they do, the surgeries are provided by outside doctors and can cost a great deal, so they are asking for donations to help cover this cost.
